---

## What this Repository is

An evidence-guided clinical navigation system for families of children and youth with disabilities, and the professionals who support them. It organises research evidence on five concepts — Participation, Family Support, Parenting Stress, Family Functioning, and Family Quality of Life — and their relationships.

---

## ✅ Can answer

- Associations between family factors and child participation
- Evidence strength and known limits (Moderate / Limited / Field-level Gap)
- Clinical prioritisation when multiple factors are present
- Conceptual questions (what is FQOL, what is Family Functioning)
- Cross-concept navigation (shared leverage points across Hubs)

---

## ❌ Cannot answer

- Child-level clinical questions (therapy type, medication, prognosis)
- Diagnosis and assessment interpretation
- Specific intervention protocols
- Legal, financial, and service navigation
- Questions outside the evidence base (see Population table below)

---

## 🏥 When to see a professional

See a doctor / clinical team when: health or safety at risk, diagnosis needed, condition changing significantly.
See OT / PT / ST when: individualised intervention plan, specific skill or environment goals.
See family counsellor / SW when: severe family conflict, mental health crisis, service navigation needed.

---

## △ When evidence is insufficient

Repository will say so explicitly when:
- Evidence is **Limited** (one or two studies)
- **Temporal gaps** exist (association known, direction over time not established)
- **Intervention gaps** exist (factors related, no trial has tested changing one improves the other)
- **Field-level Evidence Gap** (research community has not yet studied this)
- Question falls **outside current scope**

Honest "we don't know yet" is a feature, not a failure.

## Population coverage

| Population | Coverage |
|---|---|
| Children with disabilities, ages 5–17 | Primary |
| CP families | Strong (SPARCLE longitudinal) |
| ASD families | Good (cross-sectional; longitudinal limited) |
| ID families | Moderate |
| Ages 0–3 | Limited (GAP-012) |
| Adolescent transition to adult services | Limited (GAP-013) |
| Adults with disabilities | Not covered |
| Acquired disability | Not covered (GAP-014) |
| Multilingual / non-Western families | Limited (GAP-015) |
| Dual-disability families | Limited (GAP-016) |
