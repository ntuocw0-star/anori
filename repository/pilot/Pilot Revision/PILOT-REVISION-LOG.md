## New Gap Log (from Pilot)

### GAP-008
**Date:** 2026-07-13 · Sprint 33
**Triggered by:** CASE-003
**Gap Type:** Communication Support Gap (new category)
**Description:** After receiving correct evidence, users may not know how to use that evidence in real interpersonal conversations (e.g., with a partner, a family member, a school). The Repository provides knowledge and clinical guidance, but not communication scaffolding for lay users translating evidence into dialogue.
**Example:** Parent received SPARCLE finding about PS × Participation. Understood it. But said: "I don't know how to talk to my husband about this."
**Impact:** High for parent users; Medium for clinician users (who already have communication skills)
**Proposed action:** Consider adding a "How to share this" optional block to Template v2.0 for parent-facing answers — 2–3 sentence prompts for common relational contexts.
**Priority:** ★★★
**Resolved:** Open
