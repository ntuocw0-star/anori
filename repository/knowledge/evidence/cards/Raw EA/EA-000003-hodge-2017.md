## Variables

Family Leisure（两条独立路径）：

## Effect

| 模型 | 效应 | 显著性 |
|------|------|--------|
| Family Leisure Participation → FQoL | Small to moderate | Significant |
| Family Leisure Satisfaction → FQoL | Small to moderate | Significant |

**Moderators:** 婚姻状况和国籍对 Leisure Satisfaction → FQoL 的效应量有调节作用。
