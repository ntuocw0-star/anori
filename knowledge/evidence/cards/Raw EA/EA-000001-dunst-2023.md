## Notes

- This is the first Evidence Card in the Anori KOS to trigger Stopping Rule and support a direct upgrade to High.
- Outcome measurement alignment with VAR-000004 is based on the use of Beach Center Family Quality of Life Scale (or equivalent validated FQoL instruments) as the operationalization of Family Well-Being in Dunst (2023). This alignment should be noted in any future audit.
- Informal vs. Formal support effects were reported separately. Formal support → FQoL (r = .13, p = .002) is weaker and supports a separate REL (REL-000059), not this card.
- REL-000026 includes VAR-000015 (Family Functioning) as a mediator. Dunst (2023) does not test this mediation path directly. The mediator is not contradicted but also not confirmed by this evidence.
